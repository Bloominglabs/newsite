This project pursues the goal of a new primary web presence for Bloominglabs. Currently, the website for Bloominglabs (bloominglabs.org) is in the form of a wiki, mixing both public image and organizational information relevant to members of the public, with broad spectrum technical details on member projects, space equipment, and organization governance. It is desired that these functions be separated, with a clean, targeted main site, focused on conveying the essential information about Bloominglabs to the public at large, and a comprehensive wiki containing all the existing pages not converted into main site content. There is one prior effort at a new public main site, http://www.dev.bloominglabs.org/, that may be used as a low priority reference; makevention.org also provides some aesthetic/stylistic cues. However, this effort is substantially greenfield, and should be approached without prejudice to any existing codebase - far more critical is the preservation of existing site content, and its transferral to either the new wiki or new main site.

The hosting method of choice for the main site is github pages, and for the wiki, the newsite repo wiki. However, a copy of the current wiki should be contained separately in the repo, should a different new wiki host be decided upon.

Please follow the development-practices guidelines document in your execution of this task. Good fortune to you.
